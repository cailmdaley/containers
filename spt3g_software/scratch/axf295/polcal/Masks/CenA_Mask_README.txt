This folder contains CenA Masks split by map resolution. These masks are of a certain square size, and are to be used in SN calculations, polarization angle fitting and map background calculations.

Masks include:

    Upper (Northern) Lobe Mask
    Lower (Southern) Lobe Mask
    Nucleus Mask 
    Per-band T<1mK Mask (masks out anything below abs(T) < 1 mK)
